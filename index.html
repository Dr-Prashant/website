About Me
Dr. Prashant received the Ph.D. in Management in 2010, from Nagpur University, Nagpur. From 1997 till present he worked as a Assitant Professor at J.M. Patel College, in Bhandara where he started his carrer as Assitant Professor in subject of Management. He have 23 years of extensive experience in academics, He has also worked with Dept. of Management Science, KITS, Ramtek. He is specialized in subjects like Financial Account, Financial Management, Cost Accounting, Quantitative Techniques, Operation Research, etc. He has supvised Ten PhD. student till date. He has present several Research Papers in National/Indernational Seminars & Conferences.
